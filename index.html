Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, the website is free to use. You can access a wide range of lessons, exercises, and quizzes at no cost. However, we also provide premium features for users who want advanced learning resources.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No, you don’t need an account to start learning. But creating a free account helps you save your progress, track your learning journey, and unlock personalized recommendations.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

You can build your vocabulary by practicing daily lessons, using flashcards, and reading the example sentences provided with each word. We also include synonyms, pronunciations, and exercises to make learning easier.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, after completing structured premium courses, you will receive a digital certificate. This certificate can be shared on LinkedIn, added to your resume, or used to showcase your skills.
